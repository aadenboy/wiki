Hello! Thanks for reading about me!

— Greg

Greg is a beige-colored quadrupedal cag who was the second cat to appear in The Lore, right after his brother George. His appearance is based on George's, with two dark, horizontal stripes on his back and the tip of his tail, and his underbelly has a lighter color.

Personality

In contrast to George, Greg is highly sociable, socially adept, and eager to be involved. Greg likes to learn about the interests and hobbies of everyone he meets, often encouraging George to join along. He's content most of the time, and is optimistic at his core, yet will be the quickest to tell when something is wrong and attempt to sort it out.

Characteristics

His speech bubble is the common ball-and-stick shape, with the stick being a triangle that points back to him. His handwriting is less rigid and more free-flowing.

Style guide

Greg has a more browner color compared to George, with room for variation. The ear canals are not mandatory and can be omitted. The stripes and ear canals are darkened with a 40% opacity black, and underbelly lightened with a 40% opacity white. Greg is strictly quadrupedal.
