Chester is a blue polypedal cag, and partners with Linda. His head has the shape of having a snout, and his tail is extremely large and fluffy.

Personality

Chester is calm and easygoing, usually in his own world when not actively engaged. He listens to a variety of music in his free time, with his favorite genre being alternative rock. He is fond of the interests of others and will eagerly learn about what others have to offer. He will seldom attempt to compose music, but doesn't feel confident enough in his abilities to publish any at the moment.

Characteristics

Unlike most cags, Chester's head has a visible protruded region, as if he had a snout. Due to artwork Linda had made of him and their unanimous decision to make it canon, Chester is able to swap between a quadrupedal and bipedal form.

His speech bubble is the simple ball-and-stick model, with the outline only present on the connectors. The tip of the connector is an arrow. His handwriting is wider, and his arcs sometimes don't connect fully.

Style guide

Depsite the head shape, it is still round, and the protrusion does not extend far. The eyes should rest just above the start of the area, and the mouth near or at the lower portion. His tail, while incredibly fluffy, is still thick in diameter, and has a dense texture to it.
