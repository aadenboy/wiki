George is a beige-colored quadrupedal cag who was the first character to appear in The Lore. He has two dark, horizontal stripes on his back and the tip of his tail, and his underbelly has a lighter color. He is brothers with Greg.

Personality

George is an introverted, authoritative person. He will usually stick by himself rather than interact with others, however doesn't mind hanging out with Greg or Josh, and is more increasingly meeting the newer set of characters in The Lore. Unlike the norms of The Hub, he will choose not to switch his art style, as he fears that doing so will cause him to be misrepresented when meeting other characters. Despite his fear, he finds walking around and exploring the hub to be relaxing, and in turn practices rock climbing.

Personality shift

George has had one personality shift. His previous personality was more mischievous and his dislike for switching art styles was a mix of both "being meaner" and what could be described as disgust.

Characteristics

His speech bubble is the common ball-and-stick shape, with the stick being a triangle that points back to him. His handwriting ia careful and deliberate.

Style guide

When drawing George, if you wish to represent him accurately, try to replicate Aaden's art style as close as possible. His colors all derive from a single tan-orange color, with his top stripes and ear canals darkened with 40% opacity black, and underbelly lightened with 40% opacity white. George is strictly quadrupedal.
