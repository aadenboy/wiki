Kyle is a gray bipedal cag. He has very tall ears and, unlike most cags, three-fingered hands and feet. His eyes are also large, comparable to Sauce's.

Personality

Kyle is outgoing and energetic, with a love for and ability to play well in all sports. He often will take the role of a hype-man, encouraging others into events, and will also easily become competitive for the fun of it. He also has a deep interest in acting and stand-up comedy.

Characteristics

His eyes resemble Sauce's own large eyes, however don't seem to experience the same downsides.

His speech bubble is stylized like closed captions.

Style guide

Remember that cags do not have paw pads.
