Tim is a steel blue quadrupedal cag. He has three dark stripes on his forehead, and his back and underbelly are similarly colored. His body is similar to George and Greg.

Personality

Tim is timid, but will often chat with others to relax himself.

Characteristics

His speech bubble is the common ball-and-stick shape, with the stick being a triangle that points back to him. His handwriting is careful and deliberate, but often compact.
