Matthew is a gray quadrupedal cag. His appearance is intentionally obscured, either being downscaled or JPEG compressed. Unobscured, his fur is a deeply desaturated blue, and the text "1080p" is shown on his back.

Personality

Matthew primarily keeps to himself a lot. He's not content with anyone, and seems to avoid all of the Aadens whenever possible.

Characteristics

He is unable to make any discernible sound apart from compressed and often bitcrushed noises.
