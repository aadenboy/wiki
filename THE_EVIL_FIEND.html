THE EVIL FIEND is a small, purple quadrupedal cag. She is a kitten with a small, plump body and stubbed legs and tail. Her ears are short and she has large pupils.

Personality

THE EVIL FIEND is extremely antagonistic and destructive, often simply for fun or sport. Contrasting her young age, she is highly intelligent and perceptive, being easily able to deceive and outsmart others and get what she needs—partly due to the insurmountable amount of items she has. Most characters recognize this and stay clear of her out of intimidation, but she is able to have constructive conversations when she isn't actively committing to any atrocities, though this is also used as a vector towards manipulation. Despite detesting Linda, she will typically avoid harming her and Chester due to her level-headedness and formality with her at a younger age. With Sauce, she had originally taken advantage of his malleable form when he was not as intelligent, leading him to have a negative reaction towards her—she knows this fully well and spends a greater amount of her time tormenting Sauce with her presence and abilities. She is partners in crime with Marcus, but often prefers working solo.

She wears a spiked collar both from personal satisfaction and intimidation. In addition to this, she seems to have access to an infinite array of items, of which she can easily use to her advantage.

Personality shifts

Her earliest depictions show her as a young child with a fleeting mentality. More modern depictions show her instead as having a much more mature and stable observation, though there are bouts of sassiness.

Characteristics

Her overall body composition is ambiguous, but she is able to grasp objects and manipulate precise input methods easily.

Her speech bubble is a simple connector. Her handwriting is somewhat off-kilter.

Style guide

Her body should be depicted as a set of sphere or circles, with the torso and head being slightly oblong. When drawing her eyes, the pupils should encompass most of it, leaving only a crescent of the sclera visible.
