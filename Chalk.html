Chalk is a ultraviolet-colored quadrupedal cag with a gelatinous body, a brother to Sauce, Granola, Bismuth, and Glass. His body, while round, is neat and consistent in shape, primarily with cylindrical legs, and circular blank eyes.

Personality

Chalk is mostly unreactive and foggy-minded, usually indifferent to or completely unaware of what happens around him, usually to a severely delayed extent. He tends to have difficulty grasping concepts and is slow to process when he does understand, and due to his body, chooses to act in the easiest manner possible.

Characteristics

His body is defined as being an ultraviolet color. Because this is outside of human and cag vision, he is simply portrayed as an indiscernibly dark violet. Whether or not he only reflects or constantly emits ultraviolet light is left ambiguous.

His body is composed of an extremely elastic, bouncy material which resists deforming, springing back with tremendous force. He is unable to feel any pain, and is able to choose to "reset" his body at any point, instantly reverting all changes made back to normal. The inside of his body is consistent all the way through, with no organs; the only cavity being his mouth. In this way, he does not have any biological functions, and does not need to eat nor breathe. His body has a definitive upper limit on how much he can be stretched or compressed, and will shoot back with an increasingly intense amount of force. If segmented, only the largest part nearest the perceived head region remains in control. Chalk is also only able to move whatever limbs are defined in the way they normally move. His body form and mental state are independent and do not control each other.

Between his siblings, he is the most elastic at a lack of deformability.

Like Bismuth, his eyes sit flush with the surface of the head. Additionally, while he has eyelids, they don't show a visible seam where the eye "sits" in the head. His entire body, while smooth, is not wet or moist, more similar in material to a rubber ball. He can be melted and frozen, becoming more pliable or more rubbery on either extreme, at the loss of movement.

His speech bubble is the simple label model, with the text and connector colored his body color with no infill. The typeface is regular, if not somewhat jagged—the same as his handwriting. His speech itself is intentionally slurred and compressed to avoid using up energy. When writing in any medium, he also compresses his writing in a methodical manner to minimize the amount of strokes needed, such as with "Do you want..." becoming "w...".

Style guide

When drawing Chalk, avoid majorly deforming him in any way, as he isn't squishy, though he is somewhat balloon-like in deformation. His head is a sphere, while his torso, legs, and tail are cylinders. The eyes, most importantly, must be drawn flat on the head's surface, and should not protrude outwards in any way, and should not be given pupils. You should not imply any fur or texture, as his surface is smooth and featureless. If you are shading, his surface should be highly shiny and somewhat specular.
